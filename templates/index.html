Dan's Email Tester

Mailbox-level email verification

Enter any email address to check whether the mailbox actually exists and can receive mail. Verification runs a live check against the mail server. No email is ever sent.

Valid — Mailbox verified and deliverable
Risky — Address exists but may not deliver reliably (disposable, catch-all, or flagged)
Invalid — Mailbox does not exist or address is malformed
Error — Verification could not be completed
